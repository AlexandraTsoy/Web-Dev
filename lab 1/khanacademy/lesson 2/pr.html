  1. Egypt

    2. Egypt is a country with a long and exciting history. Most of its territory is covered with desert, all life takes place on the banks of the great Nile, the coasts of the Mediterranean and Red Seas. The hot climate, warm sea, ancient monuments, developed tourist infrastructure are the key to the popularity of Egypt.

    Resorts in Egypt:

    • Sharm El Sheikh, 189 196 KZT per person

      • Resort for a respectable holiday. Sharm el-Sheikh is famous for its Naama Bay, where there is a walking area with cafes, souvenir shops, clubs, discos. High-class hotels are located on the first coastline, 3 * and 4 * hotels, as a rule, on the second coastline.

    • Hurghada, 259 133 KZT per person

      • The very first resort in Egypt. A variety of hotels, from 3 * to the "de luxe" class. Most of the hotels on the first coastline. This region is characterized by sandy beaches with rare corals. The most famous and interesting excursions in Egypt start from Hurghada: the ancient city of Luxor and the Egyptian pyramids. Holidays in Hurghada are considered more economical than in Sharm el-Sheikh, more suitable for families.

  2. Mexico

    3. A bright and original country with a rich history. Mexico attracts not only with its vast cultural heritage, but also with its fantastic landscapes and long sandy beaches, friendly people and excellent cuisine. Combining rest for the soul and rest for the body is as easy as shelling pears, there are all the possibilities for this. A lot of impressions and great photos will delight you for a long time.

    Resorts in Mexico:

    • Riviera Maya, 1 502 294 KZT per person

      • A long line of sandy beaches with a total length of about a hundred kilometers, a good hotel base, a variety of entertainment, on the beaches - a convenient entry into the water. It is pleasant here to relax with children, and to spend holidays together or with a big cheerful company.

    • Cancun, 1 123 563 KZT per person

      • Cancun is not in vain considered the most popular resort in Mexico. The beach is 25 kilometers long and a huge number of discos, bars and all kinds of entertainment. The most beautiful coast with clear water and white soft sand. Excellent weather for sunbathing and surfing at almost any time of the year.
