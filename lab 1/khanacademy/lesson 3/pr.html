Lemon Pie

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Butter (softened) 120 g
Sugar 100-120 g
Wheat flour/Flour 120 g
Chicken egg 2 pcs
Lemon (depending on size) 0.5-1 pc
Baking powder 7 g

Step 1: Mix softened butter with sugar
Step 2: Add 2 eggs one at a time
Step 3: Then add flour. Stir everything thoroughly and stir in the zest and juice of half a lemon (you can add the juice of a whole lemon)
Step 4: Then add baking powder, mix thoroughly
Step 5: Cover the baking dish with paper (I have a diameter of 22-23 cm). Lay out the dough, flatten.
Step 6: Bake in a preheated oven at 190 degrees until golden brown (about 20 minutes), but keep an eye on your oven. Time may vary depending on the oven.
Step 7: Cool the cake a little, take it CAREFULLY out of the mold. If desired, you can prepare the glaze: beat lemon juice (4 tablespoons) and 150 g of powdered sugar until a white viscous mass, pour over the cake.

French Meat

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Potato 1.5 kg
Beef 750 g
Bulb onion (large) 2 pcs
Hard cheese 200 g
Mayonnaise 150 g
Salt 1 tblsp
Black pepper 1/3 tsp
Garlic 3 clove
Seasoning (for chops to taste) 1/3 tbsp

Step 1: Cut into pieces, about 1.5 -2 cm thick and the size of chops. We beat off on both sides, salt, pepper, garlic, season!
Step 2: We clean the potatoes, cut them into thin rings, salt a little, add a full spoon of mayonnaise, mix. Onion cut into thin rings.
Step 3: We spread on a baking sheet (no need to grease with oil) the first layer of potatoes, sprinkle with onions and a little grated cheese.
Step 4: Lay the second layer of potatoes on top and also sprinkle with onions and cheese. We cover our beauty with meat. Sprinkle with onions. Coat generously with mayonnaise.
Step 5: And cheese! Everything is simple! Cover the baking sheet with foil and put in the oven for an hour and a half on medium heat. Who likes rosy, remove the foil in 15 minutes! Bon appetit!

Pizza in a pan

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Wheat flour/Flour 400 g
Yeast (fresh) 15
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Water 250 ml
Tomato paste 2 tbsp
Tomato 0.5 kg
Hard cheese 0.3 kg
Basil (dried) 1 tbsp
Oregano (dried) 1 tbsp
Salt (to taste) 1/4 tbsp

Step 1: Knead the dough. While it is rising, thinly slice the tomatoes, grate the cheese (Parmesan is better, but I have the usual Tilsiter in my village). Or whatever you like. Dilute the tomato paste 2 to 1 with the juice left over from cutting the tomatoes. The dough has already doubled in size. Divide into 6-7 parts. Roll out each on a floured board into a thin pancake the diameter of your frying pan.
Step 2: We will fry our pie in olive oil, on a small fire. But before lowering the heat, warm the entire pan well. Turn over after 1-2 minutes.
Step 3: Lubricate with tomato paste, sprinkle with dried oregano and basil, then tomatoes and cheese. After 2 minutes the pizza is ready.
